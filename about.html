Learn More About Us

The Smile Foundation is a South African non-governmental organization that assists children with any facial conditions.

We help children in need of plastic and reconstructive surgery for conditions like cleft lip and palate.

Heading title

Our mission is to ensure that every child in South Africa living with facial paralysis, cleft lip, cleft palate, craniofacial abnormalities, burn wounds, or ear, nose, and hand conditions receives the specialised care they need to live a healthier, happier life.
